How to get Play Protect

As mentioned earlier, the Play Protect is part of the Play store. so, if you have the Play store on your phone, as soon as this apps gets updated, you will get the Play protect on your phone. Considering this is Google we are talking about, it might be a few days, or even weeks, before the Play Protect is added to you Play store app.


Once the Protect feature has been added you will be able to access it through:

-- Open the Play app on your phone

-- Map on the menu, which are three lines on the left of the screen. From menu, select Protect.
